APPLICATION PROCESS

While many people will start this journey, yours will be unique to you. A school or college can at best be an enabler, but the rest is up to you. If you have the right attitude, and are willing to put in the work that's required, we will support you all the way through. Your journey with us will start the day you get in touch with us and continue through graduation - and beyond.

You can start your application process by filling out the application form. You'll have a dedicated counselor to guide you and your family through the application process. They'll advise you on finances, scholarships, campus details, and how to apply. If at any time during the process, you are unable to get your query resolved, you can drop an email to applicant.support@itm.edu with the subject line clearly stating which program you are applying to.
