Value-Added Professional Certifications

SAS-Certified Advanced Analytics Professional

The SAS-Certified Advanced Analytics Professional certification highlights advanced proficiency in using SAS software for complex statistical analysis. This certification demonstrates the ability to perform sophisticated data manipulations, predictive modeling, and decision-making support using SAS tools. Professionals with this certification can extract meaningful insights from intricate datasets and apply advanced analytical techniques to drive data-informed strategies and decisions.

Statistical Business Analyst

The Statistical Business Analyst certification verifies expertise in applying statistical methods to business challenges. This certification emphasizes applying statistical techniques to analyze business data, enabling organizations to make informed, data-driven decisions. Certified individuals are skilled in conducting rigorous analyses to optimize business strategies and enhance organizational performance through statistical insights.

Enterprise-Scale Analytics Solutions with Microsoft Azure and Power BI

The Enterprise-Scale Analytics Solutions with Microsoft Azure and Power BI certification demonstrates proficiency in designing and implementing large-scale analytics solutions using Microsoft Azure and Power BI. This certification showcases the ability to integrate data from multiple sources, leveraging cloud-based analytics and business intelligence tools to generate comprehensive and actionable business insights. Professionals certified in this area can develop scalable analytics solutions that support strategic decision-making and business growth.

Building Modern Python App on AWS

The Building Modern Python Apps on AWS certification indicates expertise in developing and deploying modern applications using Python within the Amazon Web Services (AWS) environment. This certification highlights skills in cloud computing, application development, and creating scalable solutions. Certified professionals are proficient in utilizing AWS services to build, deploy, and manage Python applications, emphasizing cloud-based solutions and scalable infrastructure.

Tableau Desktop Qualified Associate Certification

The Tableau Desktop Qualified Associate Certification verifies the ability to use Tableau for effective data visualization. This certification focuses on creating interactive dashboards, analyzing data, and deriving actionable insights. Certified individuals are skilled in leveraging Tableau's features to present complex data in an accessible and visually engaging manner, supporting informed decision-making through interactive and informative visual representations.

Google Analytics 4

The Google Analytics 4 certification demonstrates expertise in utilizing Google Analytics 4 for tracking and analyzing web and app data. This certification focuses on understanding user behavior, measuring digital marketing performance, and optimizing marketing strategies based on data insights. Certified professionals can leverage Google Analytics 4 to gain comprehensive insights into user interactions and enhance digital marketing effectiveness.

GLOBAL IMMERSION PROGRAMS

In collaboration with international universities and corporate houses, ITM offers the Global Immersion Programs (GIP) at four locations for this program: UAE, Malaysia, Singapore, and Europe. Students are encouraged to participate because the experience provides an opportunity to interact with global industry leaders and exposure to multicultural environments.

GIP at UAE (4 Days), University of Dubai

Hosted by the University of Dubai

Enhance your knowledge with sessions at the University of Dubai. Learn from top faculty and industry professionals, explore innovative labs, and join interactive workshops, gaining practical insights into current industry trends within a dynamic academic setting.

INDUSTRY VISITS

  • Nasdaq Dubai: Dive into the dynamic world of finance with an exclusive visit to Nasdaq Dubai, the leading international stock exchange in the Middle East, offering insights into global trading and market operations.
  • Apple Inc.: Explore the forefront of innovation at Apple Dubai, where cutting-edge technology and sleek design converge. Witness firsthand how Apple revolutionizes the tech industry from the heart of this vibrant city.

EXCURSIONS

  • Visit to Palm Jumeirah: Discover the architectural marvel of Palm Jumeirah, an iconic man-made island that epitomizes luxury and innovation in Dubai.
  • Dubai City Immersion Tour: Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and futuristic skyline of Dubai with our comprehensive city tour, showcasing the best of this dynamic metropolis.
  • Cultural Exploration featuring Bur Dubai, Dhow Cruise, and Desert Safari: Experience the rich heritage of Dubai with a cultural tour that includes the historic Bur Dubai, a scenic Dhow Cruise, and an adventurous Desert Safari.
  • Palm Atlantis & Burj Al Arab: Marvel at the opulence of Dubai with visits to Palm Atlantis and Burj Al Arab, two of the most luxurious and iconic landmarks in the world.
  • Burj Khalifa: Stand in awe of the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, and enjoy breathtaking views of Dubai's stunning skyline from its observation decks.
