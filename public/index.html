BBA

After successfully teaching the MBA program (earlier known as PGDM) for over three decades, on popular demand, we launched the Bachelor of Business Administration (ITM BBA ùíäConnect ) program in 2024. Based on the learnings from the MBA program, the program is designed for real -life success.

Designed completely by industry professionals, our ITM BBA ùíäConnect is a 3-year full-time program (with an optional 4th year for a BBA Honors degree) emphasizing practical, hands-on learning. Unlike traditional BBA, we prioritize industry connections and experiential learning over theoretical classroom study. Assessment is based on internships, a capstone project, industrial visits to Singapore and Dubai, case studies, and overall readiness for the real-world challenges of business.
