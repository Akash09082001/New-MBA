Central to the ITM Business School is ùíäConnect , or 'Industry Connect'. As a part of the ùíäConnect initiative, ITM organizes a range of campus engagement & outbound activities year round for connecting recruiting organizations and students, which include:

Internships

The five-month Industry Internship program at ITM aims to immerse students in real organizational environments, exposing them to roles guided by company mentors and faculty. This experience enhances the multidimensional skill sets they developed during their first year and is facilitated by our dedicated placement department.

NGO Internships

The NGO internship program at ITM focuses on enhancing students' Emotional Social Quotient (ESQ) by engaging them in 15-day projects with NGOs, trusts, charitable institutions, or CSR departments. It aims to broaden their perspective on management decisions humanely and sensitize them to social welfare roles and management practices within NGOs.

Live Projects

ITM offers students 10 live projects per specialization, allowing them to gain hands-on experience crucial for career readiness. These projects provide real-world exposure and extensive industry networking opportunities, significantly boosting employability and practical skills during final placements.

Leadership Talks

The Leadership Talks at ITM bridge academia and the corporate world by inviting industry leaders to share their experiences. Students gain insights into industry trends, expand their networks, and learn from the success stories and challenges these leaders face, inspiring them to pursue professional growth.

Masterclasses & Workshops

Designed to nurture employability, masterclasses, and workshops at ITM cover entrepreneurship essentials such as market analysis, financial planning, and business model development. Senior industry executives conduct these sessions, preparing students with practical knowledge and skills for the techno-industrial future.

One-on-one Mentorship

ITM's one-on-one mentorship fosters a supportive environment where faculty mentors guide students at every step of their academic journey, providing personalized support and encouragement.

Hackathons & Competitions

Participation in hackathons and competitions at ITM encourages students to think creatively and develop solutions for real-world challenges, enhancing their critical thinking skills and practical problem-solving abilities.

Industry Visits

ITM organizes industry visits that allow students to engage directly with industry leaders, gaining firsthand experience in team management and navigating business complexities. These visits expose students to multicultural environments and diverse business practices.

Industry Conferences

Industry conferences at ITM allow students to engage deeply with current industry trends and network with professionals. These events broaden their understanding of the business landscape and facilitate discussions on emerging challenges and opportunities in various sectors.

PERSONAL PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT: TALEWIND

A SOFT SKILL DEVELOPMENT INITIATIVE

At ITM Business School, our TALEWIND department is essential in guiding students through their transition from academia to the corporate world. We've designed our curriculum to enhance soft skills, team dynamics, cross-cultural sensitivity, and technical proficiency in MS Office. We focus on public speaking, email and presentation skills, and Group Discussion & Personal Interview preparation. Our experienced faculty, experts in soft skills, communication, and behavioral sciences, provide personalized mentoring to help each student excel.

With 300 hours of soft skills training, we ensure students are ready for interviews and beyond, covering everything from cultural sensitivity to technical skills. This includes training in video presentations, interviews, resume building, and case-based group discussions, with a focus on creating resumes that stand out in Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). We also facilitate interactions with alumni, providing valuable insights into industry expectations and inspiring career goals.
