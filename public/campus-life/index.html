Campus life

Hostel life

Nestled in Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, a thriving "Smart City" and hub of global financial activities, our technologically advanced and wi-fi enabled, green campus provides an ideal backdrop for academic and professional growth. We recently updated the campus infrastructure and have brand-new interactive air conditioned classrooms, video conferencing facilities, auditorium and specialized labs.

Our campus has plenty of green open spaces, an amphitheater, etc. We provide our students access to the latest softwares and digital libraries. We also offer hostel facilities so students can benefit from a vibrant community. These facilities underscore our commitment to delivering both curricular and extracurricular learning experiences essential for the holistic development of our students.

Health & welness

We provide comprehensive medical, psychological, and wellness support services to our students year-round. Our Wellness Committee is dedicated to promoting the health and well-being of our students, fostering an environment that supports their learning and success.

Student club

At ITM Business School, we believe in nurturing the holistic development of our students. Our diverse range of student clubs provides opportunities for personal growth, skill enhancement, and networking. Whether your interests lie in culture, technology, entrepreneurship, or wellness, there's a club for everyone. Below, you'll find a brief overview of each of our dynamic student clubs.

Campus Events

On campus events are a vibrant showcase of our dynamic community, offering students diverse opportunities to learn, connect, and grow. From cross-cultural celebrations like Melange to competitive fests like U-Fest, each event reflects our commitment to fostering creativity, collaboration, and inclusivity. These experiences enrich campus life, allowing students to explore their passions, develop essential skills, and build lasting memories in a supportive and energetic environment.

Cultural Events & Festivals

At ITM Business School, we celebrate cultural events and festivals, fostering a strong sense of community and mutual respect. These vibrant gatherings bring students together, offering a platform to honor and share diverse traditions. By participating in these celebrations, students deepen their understanding of different cultures, build lasting connections, and enrich their campus experience with meaningful, shared moments.
